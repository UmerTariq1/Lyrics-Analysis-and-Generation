Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift, born on December 13, 1989, gained fame in the mid-2000s. Her most famous album, "1989" (2014), marked a shift to pop. Her most recent album is "Fearless (Taylor's Version)" (2021), and she is currently 33 years old.

Eminem

Eminem, born Marshall Bruce Mathers III on October 17, 1972, became a rap icon in the late 1990s. His most famous album, "The Marshall Mathers LP" (2000), is a seminal work in hip-hop. His most recent album, "Music to Be Murdered By" (2020), continues to showcase his enduring influence as a rap legend at the age of 50.
